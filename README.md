# Madlibs--website
A fun and interactive Mad Libs game where you can fill in the blanks to create a funny story!

Features:
Fill in words (like name, adjective, verb) and see them appear in a story + The story is displayed with a cool typewriter effect.
How to Play: Fill in the blanks with words+Click Submit Your Words to see your story
